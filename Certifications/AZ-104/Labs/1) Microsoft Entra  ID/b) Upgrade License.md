### Steps to Upgrade Your License

- 1.1
    
    In your browser, go to: [https://portal.azure.com](https://portal.azure.com)
    
- 1.2
    
    In the **Search bar**, type and select **Microsoft Entra ID** (formerly Azure Active Directory)

- 1.3
    
    In the left-hand menu, click **Licenses**
    
- 1.4
    
    Select **All products**
    
- 1.5
    
    If applicable, click **+ Try/Buy**, then **Activate** for any available trials
    

> ‚ö†Ô∏è _This step may not be necessary depending on your organization or sandbox._

- 1.6

### If You Only See External Identities

- You may not have the right permissions (such as **Global Admin**).
- Your tenant might be a **limited sandbox** or **B2C-restricted** tenant.
- If you're trying to enable external access, use **Microsoft Entra External ID**:
    - In the portal, search for **Microsoft Entra External ID**
    - Review setup instructions for **external collaboration** or **customer access** models

### Troubleshooting Tip:

If you're not seeing what others see, go to **Microsoft Entra Admin Center** directly: [https://entra.microsoft.com](https://entra.microsoft.com)
