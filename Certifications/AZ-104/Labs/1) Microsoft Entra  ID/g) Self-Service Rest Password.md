## Self-Service Rest Password

### Section 1: Self-Service Rest Password [Requires Premium P1, P2, or Business 365 Standard or Premium]

Note: If you don't have P1, P2, Business 365 Standard or Premium, it's not mandatory to complete as long as you understand the concept

- 1.1
    
    In your web browser visit: [https://portal.azure.com/#home](https://portal.azure.com/#home)
    
- 1.2
    
    In the Search Tab, look for Active AD (Azure Active Directory)
    
- 1.3
    
    Click on Password Reset
    

![](https://images2.imgbox.com/30/c3/OIBg8wAh_o.png)

- 1.4
    
    Click on Properties, Select the Self-service password reset enabled (**All** is recommended)
    
- 1.5
    
    Click Save
    

![](https://images2.imgbox.com/ef/14/yhHnFnZV_o.png)

- 1.6
    
    Click on Authentication methods
    
- 1.7
    
    Click on the settings preferred
    
- 1.8
    
    Select security questions
    

![](https://images2.imgbox.com/bb/c1/wCqPPG2P_o.png)

- 1.9
    
    Select Custom and create/choose questions you want, Select the Predefined questions you want
    
- 1.10
    
    Click on Ok
    

![](https://images2.imgbox.com/e8/01/M6ye9rMP_o.png)

- 1.11
    
    Click on Registration
    
- 1.12
    
    Select the options desired.
    

![](https://images2.imgbox.com/c6/cd/qMvW12jy_o.png)

- 1.13
    
    Click on Notifications
    
- 1.14
    
    Select the options desired. (Yes is recommended for both)
    

![](https://images2.imgbox.com/07/87/d0WAqvfv_o.png)

- 1.15
    
    Click on Customization
    
- 1.16
    
    Select **Yes** for Customized helpdesk link and enter in custom helpdesk email or URL
    

![](https://images2.imgbox.com/b3/9c/HFtHY6SF_o.png)

- 1.17
    
    Click on Usage and insights
    
- 1.18
    
    You can view statistics on user registration numbers and more!
    

![](https://images2.imgbox.com/6e/3b/DigWqp8w_o.png)
