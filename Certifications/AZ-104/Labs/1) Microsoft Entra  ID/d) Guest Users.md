## How to invite Guest Users

### Section 1: Guest Users

- 1.1
    
    In your web browser visit: [https://portal.azure.com/#home](https://portal.azure.com/#home)
    
- 1.2
    
    In the Search Tab, look for Active AD (Azure Active Directory)
    
- 1.3
    
    Click on the Users Tab
    

![](https://images2.imgbox.com/ae/b8/xldWqiE6_o.png)

- 1.4
    
    Click on New guest user
    

![](https://images2.imgbox.com/16/ab/1zj2Ovst_o.png)

- 1.5
    
    Select Invite user
    
- 1.6
    
    Enter in Name, Email address, First name, Last name
    
- 1.7
    
    Enter in the groups, users, etc that you want
    

![](https://images2.imgbox.com/b6/5d/82kXD9Qq_o.png)

- 1.8
    
    A new user should be included on your list as a guest
    

![](https://images2.imgbox.com/e6/26/0AwY9zjl_o.png)
